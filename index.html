Our Mission

An Association Built for Ghanaians, by Ghanaians

GHASSA — Ghanaian Students in Spain Alliance — is a non-profit association of Ghanaian students and professionals living in Spain. We offer practical support on academic, professional, and administrative matters while building a strong, warm community.

Whether you just arrived in Spain or have been here for years, GHASSA is your family away from home. We help you navigate Spanish life, connect with fellow Ghanaians, and access opportunities.

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by these principles that make GHASSA special.

🤝

Mutual Support

We help each other navigate Spanish life — from bureaucracy to finding opportunities. No one walks alone.

🌟

Community First

Building lasting connections between Ghanaians across Spain, creating friendships that go beyond borders.

📚

Education & Growth

Sharing resources, scholarships, and guidance to help every member reach their full potential in Spain.

🥁

Cultural Pride

Celebrating our Ghanaian heritage, language, and traditions — keeping our roots alive far from home.

💼

Professional Network

Connecting members with career opportunities, mentors, and professional development in Spain and beyond.

🔎

Information Access

Providing clear, up-to-date guidance on visas, studies, work rights, and administrative processes in Spain.
