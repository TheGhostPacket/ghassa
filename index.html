About GHASSA

Our Purpose: GHASSA is a vibrant community of Ghanaian students and professionals in Spain, promoting unity, mutual support, and celebration of our rich Ghanaian heritage.


We connect, support, and empower members by sharing information, opportunities, and experiences, and by providing a platform for academic, professional, and social networking.


What We Offer: Updates on academic and professional opportunities, life and culture advice, a space to network on achievements. Respect and guidelines are paramount. Be positive and engage constructively.
