Our Story

From a Shared Need to a Thriving Community

GHASSA was born out of a simple but powerful reality: Ghanaians arriving in Spain often faced the same challenges — navigating immigration paperwork, understanding the education system, finding work, and simply feeling at home in a new country.

In 2023, a group of passionate Ghanaian students and professionals decided to change that. They formed GHASSA — Ghanaian Students in Spain Alliance — to create a structured, welcoming, and resourceful community for every Ghanaian in Spain.

Today, GHASSA supports hundreds of Ghanaians across Spain with events, guides, networking, and a warm community that never lets you feel alone.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Objectives

🎯

Our Mission

To connect, support, and empower Ghanaian students and professionals in Spain through shared information, opportunities, and experiences — helping each member thrive in their new home.

🌟

Our Vision

A Spain where every Ghanaian — regardless of background or status — has access to a strong, informed community and the resources to build a successful, fulfilling life.

📋

Our Objectives

Provide academic & administrative support, facilitate professional networking, celebrate Ghanaian culture, and serve as a trusted information hub for Ghanaians across Spain.

Our Journey

GHASSA Timeline

From a small group of friends to a growing national community — here's how GHASSA was built.

2023 – Q1

The Idea is Born

A group of Ghanaian students lead by Abigail Adu Asamoah in Spain identified the need for a structured community support system and began organizing.

2023 – Q2

GHASSA Founded

GHASSA is officially registered as a non-profit organisation in Spain with its first executive team in place.

2023 – Q3

First Community Events

GHASSA hosts its first networking and orientation events, welcoming new Ghanaian students to Spain.

2023 – Q4

Resource Hub Launched

The GHASSA Kit de Becas and academic orientation resources are compiled and shared with the community.

2024 – Now

Growing Strong

Membership grows past 200, more cities are reached, and GHASSA continues expanding its support services.
